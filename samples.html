How Consistency Works

Engineering Behind the Brushwork

For each of our five signature styles, we lock down everything that drives a portrait's visual identity — and let only the pet vary.

Model

Flux Pro Kontext

A state-of-the-art image-to-image model from Black Forest Labs purpose-built to apply heavy stylistic transformations while preserving subject identity. We run it via fal.ai.

Aspect & Resolution

3:4 portrait, print-ready

Every output is generated at 3:4 aspect — the classical portrait ratio — at a resolution sufficient to print up to 24×36 inches without loss of detail.

Composition Lock

Same framing, every time

Head-and-shoulders bust. Three-quarter angle. Subject centred. Eyes in the upper third. Soft direct eye contact with the viewer. Locked into the prompt as a non-negotiable rule.

Per-Style Seed

Deterministic styling

Each style ships with a fixed numerical seed — 1492 for Renaissance, 1788 for Regal Oil, 42 for Cosmic, and so on — so the same reference photo always produces the same artwork.
