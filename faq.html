The Portrait Process
For the best results, use a clear, well-lit photo where your pet's face is the main focus. Front-facing or slight three-quarter angle shots work beautifully. Avoid blurry images, heavy shadows across the face, or photos where your pet is very far from the camera. A single pet per image produces the most detailed portrait — though we do support multi-pet photos.
Our AI is trained on thousands of classical and contemporary art styles. When you upload your photo, it analyses your pet's features — coat texture, eye shape, expression — and reimagines them in your chosen artistic style. The result is a unique, one-of-a-kind piece that captures your pet's personality while honouring centuries of artistic tradition. No two portraits are ever exactly alike.
Portrait generation typically takes 20–60 seconds depending on server load. You'll see a live progress indicator while your masterpiece is being crafted. If generation takes longer than 2 minutes, please refresh and try again — your upload will still be available.
Absolutely. You can regenerate your portrait as many times as you like before purchasing — each generation is completely free. Each attempt uses a different creative interpretation, so you may get a subtly different result each time. Only pay when you're completely happy with your portrait.
Dogs and cats produce the most detailed results, but our AI works wonderfully with rabbits, birds, hamsters, guinea pigs, horses, reptiles, and most other beloved companions. As long as there's a clear photo of your pet, we can transform them into royalty.
Artistic Styles
We currently offer five signature styles: Renaissance Royal (rich oil tones, classical regalia), Regal Oil (loose brushwork in the Dutch master tradition), Modern Minimal (clean lines, contemporary gallery aesthetic), Watercolour Whimsy (soft washes, painterly edges), and Cosmic Royalty (celestial drama, deep space atmosphere). New styles are added regularly.

Orders & Delivery
We offer three formats: Digital Download ($19) — a high-resolution 4K file delivered instantly to your email, perfect for personal printing or screen display. Printed & Framed Portrait ($79) — premium giclée print at 12×16", mounted in your chosen frame finish, delivered in 5–7 business days. Gallery Canvas (+$60 upgrade) — a hand-stretched 20×24" canvas printed on artist-grade material, ideal for statement walls.
Here's a full breakdown of how long your order will take:

Production time: Your portrait is printed and dispatched within 1–2 business days — most framed prints and wall art ship within 24–48 hours of ordering.

Australia: 3–8 business days after dispatch. Express courier options are available at checkout.

USA & Canada: 3–7 business days after dispatch.

UK & Europe: 2–5 business days after dispatch.

Rest of World: 5–14 business days after dispatch, depending on your location.

Your portrait is fulfilled via Prodigi's global print network and printed at a facility closest to you, which keeps both delivery times and shipping costs down. You'll receive a tracking link by email once your order ships. Digital downloads are delivered to your inbox within minutes of payment.
Yes, we ship worldwide. International orders are lovingly packed in protective art tubes or rigid flat-pack boxes to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country.
All prints ship from climate-controlled facilities in acid-free tissue paper, sandwiched between rigid backing boards inside a bespoke Pawtré Studio box. Framed portraits are additionally bubble-wrapped and corner-protected. We take the same care with your order as if we were shipping our own art.

Once your portrait is finalised, your order is routed to the facility closest to your shipping address. This means faster delivery, lower carbon miles, and consistent museum-grade quality wherever you are in the world.
Framed prints are produced on premium giclée fine-art paper using archival pigment inks rated for 100+ years of fade resistance under normal indoor lighting. Canvas editions are printed on artist-grade poly-cotton canvas, hand-stretched over solid wood bars and finished with a protective satin varnish.

Your Photos & Privacy
Your uploaded photo is used solely to generate your portrait. It is processed in memory and is not stored permanently on our servers after generation is complete. We do not use your pet photos to train AI models, share them with third parties, or use them for any marketing purposes without your explicit consent. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Upon purchase, you receive a personal, non-commercial licence to use your portrait for home display, gifting, and personal enjoyment. Commercial use (e.g. selling prints, merchandise, or using the portrait in advertising) requires a separate commercial licence — please contact us to discuss.
