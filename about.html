Our Story

For Every Pet Who Deserves a Palace Wall

Pawtré Studio was founded on a simple belief: the love we have for our pets is timeless — and the art we make of them should be too.

Pets aren't just companions. They're family. And family deserves to be hung in a frame, not buried in a camera roll.

Pawtré Studio began with a Labrador called Hugo, an old oil painting in an antique shop in Paris, and the small, slightly silly question — why don't we paint our pets the way 18th-century nobility painted theirs?

The answer, of course, is that commissioning a real oil portrait costs thousands and takes months. Most of us — even the most devoted pet parents — would never get there. So our beloved companions, the ones who greet us every morning and warm the foot of the bed each night, end up living only in phone galleries and Instagram stories.

We wanted to fix that.

Pawtré Studio uses the latest in AI artistry — paired with the craft of fine-art printing — to give every pet the regal portrait they deserve. Renaissance grandeur. Watercolour softness. Cosmic drama. Five carefully tuned styles, each capturing your pet's likeness with care, then printed with archival inks on museum-grade paper or canvas, framed, and delivered to your door anywhere in the world.

We aren't trying to replace real artists. We're making art accessible — to the rescue mum with three cats, to the kid whose hamster is his best friend, to the grandfather who lost his dog last spring and wants something beautiful to remember her by.

Every portrait we make is a small love letter — from you, to them, forever.

— The Pawtré Studio team
