How Fulfilment Works

Printed Locally, Delivered Globally

Pawtré Studio partners with Prodigi, a global print-on-demand network with production facilities across Australia, the UK, the EU, the USA, and Asia. When you place an order, we automatically route your portrait to the facility closest to your shipping address.

This means most international customers receive a domestic delivery — no customs hold-ups, no overseas shipping fees, and a smaller carbon footprint. Every print, regardless of facility, follows the same quality-controlled production process.

Customs, Duties & Taxes

What to Expect

Because Prodigi prints locally in most major regions, the vast majority of orders are treated as domestic shipments with no customs paperwork and no import charges. This includes orders shipped within Australia, the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and most EU member states.

For orders shipped across borders — for example, to remote regions or countries without a local Prodigi facility — any import duties, VAT/GST, or local taxes are assessed by the destination country's customs authority and are the recipient's responsibility. We have no control over these charges and cannot predict them in advance.

  • EU customers: VAT is calculated and included at checkout where required under EU IOSS rules. You won't be charged additional VAT on delivery for orders under €150.
  • UK customers: VAT is included at checkout for all orders.
  • US customers: No federal import duty on personal art purchases under US$800.
  • Australian customers: GST is included at checkout for all domestic orders.
  • All other destinations: Any local taxes are payable on delivery by the recipient.
Digital Downloads

Instant Delivery

If you've chosen the digital download option, your high-resolution portrait file is delivered to your email inbox within minutes of payment confirmation. There are no shipping fees, no customs, and no waiting — just your masterpiece, ready to print at home or display on screen.

Digital files are provided in print-ready PNG and JPG formats at 300 DPI, suitable for printing up to 24×36" without loss of quality.

Tracking Your Order

Stay Informed

You'll receive automated emails at every stage of your order:

  • Order confirmation — sent immediately after checkout.
  • In production — when your portrait enters the print queue (typically within 24 hours).
  • Dispatched — with your carrier and tracking number.
  • Delivered — confirmation once your portrait arrives.

If you haven't received a dispatch confirmation within 3 business days of ordering, please email us at hello@pawtrestudio.com and we'll investigate immediately.

Lost or Delayed Orders

Should Anything Go Wrong

While Prodigi's global network is highly reliable, occasionally parcels are delayed or lost in transit. If your order has not arrived within the expected delivery window, please contact us at hello@pawtrestudio.com with your order number. We'll investigate with the carrier and, if your order is confirmed lost, arrange a free replacement at no cost to you.

For orders confirmed as damaged on arrival, please see our Returns Policy.
