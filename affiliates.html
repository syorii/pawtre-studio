Program Terms

The Fine Print

Plain English. Fair for everyone.

  1. I Commissions are paid on completed, non-refunded orders only. If a customer refunds within our return window, the commission is reversed.
  2. II Cookie windows reset on every click. Whichever affiliate's link was clicked most recently before purchase earns the commission.
  3. III Self-referrals are not permitted. Commission on your own orders or those of immediate family members will be voided.
  4. IV No paid search on our brand terms. Bidding on "Pawtré Studio" or close variants in Google Ads, Bing, etc. is prohibited.
  5. V Disclose your affiliate relationship in line with FTC, ASA, and ACCC guidelines wherever you promote.
  6. VI No spam, no misleading claims, no incentive-traffic networks. We reserve the right to terminate affiliates who damage the brand.
  7. VII Payouts are minimum $50 USD equivalent, processed monthly via Stripe Connect or PayPal. Below threshold, balance rolls over.
